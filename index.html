THE DINO CAFÉ!

Hello and welcome to one of London's most unique cafés.
A laid back and entertaining environment, for those who want a nice meal after spending the day in the nearby museum or spend a relaxing afternoon in the company of friends with coffee or tea.

Coffee and tea are served throughout the day.
However, during the busy lunch period, it is advised to make a reservation otherwise takeaway is available.

Please let us know before you order, or make a reservation, of any allergies you or anyone in your party may have and we will always be happy to accommodate.
